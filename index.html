Style Improvement Tips

AI Editorial

Master Subtle Textures

Avoid plain flat polyester fabrics. Layer textured heavy-knit cotton, waffle weaves, and brushed linen to inject sophisticated depth into solid black outfits.

Rule of Thirds Proportion

Tuck undercoats and adjust trouser rises to create a visual 1:2 height ratio. This elongates legs and builds a highly attractive editorial silhouette.

Anchor with Contrast

When wearing monochromatic black or deep purple coordinates, break up outlines using premium accessories like a silver curb chain or high-sole white sneakers.
